About

I'm Babak Lotfalizadeh, an accomplished Mobile Software Developer with over 10 years of experience, specializing in Flutter (5 years) and Native Mobile Application development (5 years). I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from Payam Noor University and have a proven history of building successful apps across various industries.

I currently work as a Senior Flutter Developer at the European Broadcasting Union (EBU), where I develop and maintain Flutter applications and improve data flow through API integration with internal systems. Previously, I've contributed to leading companies including SKIMS, Floward, Geeks LTD, Raimun, and Tahlilgaran.

My functional expertise spans Flutter & Dart development, state management (Provider, Riverpod, Bloc, GetX), API integration, Firebase, CI/CD pipelines, automated testing, performance optimization, and Agile methodologies. I'm proficient in Kotlin, Java, JavaScript, React Native, and have experience with Firebase, Firestore, REST APIs, and more.

I demonstrate strong technical prowess, a professional work ethic, and excellent communication skills. I'm always excited to take on new challenges and build high-quality, maintainable mobile applications.
